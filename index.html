About

I am a Graduate student at the University of Illinois at Chicago, concentrating on hardware implementations, hardware security, machine learning, and computer organization. I started my journey in this field at the Indian Institute of Information Technology Guwahati, where I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering and have a long way to go.
