About

I'm a passionate Ph.D. student at University of Illinois with a strong focus on hardware implementations, hardware security, machine learning, and computer organization. My journey in the field began at IIITG, where I earned my Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering. I'm dedicated to advancing technology and solving complex challenges in the world of hardware technology. I am a fast learner with a wide range of interest fields and diverse hobbies.

Whether I'm traveling, delving into science, or simply reflecting, I embrace every experience with a curious mind and a desire to grow.
